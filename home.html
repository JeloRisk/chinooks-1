CHINOOKS

It was January 2014 when Chinooks Roast & Fried Chicken was first introduced in the market. Founded by a Chinese Entrepreneur named Mr. Bernard Chua, the brand was launched as a mom-and-pop set up selling fried chicken in a busy street of Malabon City


In the year 2018, the owner began to offer roasted chicken. The simple idea behind the name CHINOOKS was the word combination of "Chinese and Manok" and Roast & Fried Chicken as we offer both fried and roasted chicken.

To become one of Favorite and most recognized roasted chicken company around the world. A roasted chicken of chinooks is what we are introduce.
