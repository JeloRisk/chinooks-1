Company Profile


It was January 2014 when Chinooks Roast & Fried Chicken was first introduced in the market. Founded by a Chinese Entrepreneur named Mr. Bernard Chua, the brand was launched as a mom-and-pop set up selling fried chicken in a busy street of Malabon City. In the year 2018, the owner began to offer roasted chicken. The simple idea behind the name CHINOOKS was the word combination of "Chinese and Manok" and Roast & Fried Chicken as we offer both fried and roasted chicken. Proud to say that we are one the pioneers who provides Chinese-style roasted chicken in the metro. In just a short span of time, Chinooks had evolved and further improved the business by branching out to neighbor locations in Metro Manila to cater more customers with its existing flavors and twist of the Chinese style that everyone loves to rellish. To date, our brand aims to grow through franchising and be able to share our secret success as a chickenm business that roasts with passion for our dfuture business partners.
